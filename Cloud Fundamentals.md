**What is cloud?**  
  Cloud (Cloud Computing) means using someone else’s servers over the internet instead of using our own local server. Instead of buying and
  maintaining physical hardware, you rent computing resources like server, ram, ssd, hdd etc. It is the **on demand** delivery of resources
  over the internet with **pay-as-you-go** pricing.

**What are the problems cloud is solving?**  
  Instead of buying expensive hardwares, rent data center space, install networking, hire people to maintain everything, we can take the
  services from providers and pay only for what we use. We dont need invest big at a single time.
  In short, Cloud solves High cost, low scalability, slow deployment, and infrastructure complexity.
